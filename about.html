 About us

Know about us

Vignan University, Guntur is a private deemed university which was established in the academic year of 2008. The University is located in Guntur district of Andhra Pradesh. The university was formerly known as Vignan's Engineering College founded in the year 1997. The motto of the university is to provide high quality education by academic programs, training activities, and research facilities
